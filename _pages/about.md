Welcome to my website!

I am a macroeconomist, with a general interest in macroeconomic dynamics. In particular, I am interested in the role of expectations and heterogeneity for business cycle fluctuations. I am also interested in solution methods for economic models.

I am a Senior Economist in the Research-Monetary Policy Department at [Danmarks Nationalbank](https://www.nationalbanken.dk/en).
